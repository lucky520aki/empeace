Our Projects

Following are the projects that we currently working on.

Cryptocurrency Mining

Instead of mining Bitcoin which requires massive hardware investments, we are currently mining altcoins such as Ethereum and Monero.

Technology

In order to achive the best work, we need to maximize the mining efficiency and minimize the power consumption.

Finance

We will be using these cryptocurrencies mined to pay our executive compensations and employee salaries.

Software Development

One of the services we are developing is the service that digitalizes the paper receipts - to provide retailers the customer analytics and to provide users with the data visualization for personal finance.

Technology

We take advantage of the frameworks such as React Native and Flutter which enable us to achieve rapid cross-platform deployment. As for the programming language, we mainly use Javascript, Dart, Python.

Finance

We intend to make our services available for average joes. Hence, there would be no fees for regular users.
